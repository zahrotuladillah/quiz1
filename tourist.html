Ngebel Lake

There are many mystical stories about Ngebel Lake, but the exotic natural scenery is much more enjoyable to enjoy.

This natural lake that is located at the foot of Mount Wilis stretches a beautiful landscape and is pleasant to see. The natural nuance also eliminates the mystical atmosphere that comes from the stories of the surrounding community.


Location : Ngebel District, Ponorogo Regency, East Java
Mloko Sewu

For those who are interested in visiting tourist attractions in Ponorogo that are complete and offer special natural charm and Instagrammable photo spots, Mloko Sewu is the destination you are going to.

Take note of the various charms, ranging from flower gardens, tree houses, sky doors to selfie spots with views of Ngebel Lake from a height, you can't miss the sun.


Location : Prumbon, Pupus, Ngebel, Ponorogo Regency, East Java
Pletuk Waterfall

One of the waterfalls in Ponorogo that you can visit to get a natural and amazing feel of nature is Pletuk Waterfall.

It's a bit hidden and it takes a bit of effort to get to the location. But upon arrival at this waterfall, you will be treated to a natural atmosphere with a waterfall that has a heavy water discharge and green natural scenery. In addition to bathing in the river, you can also do extreme activities such as rock climbing.


Location: Kranggan, Jurug, Sooko, Setumbal, Jurug, Sooko, Ponorogo Regency, East Java
Beruk Mountain

Those who want to relax with views of the typical Ponorogo mountain slopes with their cool landscapes can visit Mount Beruk.

This tourist spot in Ponorogo offers a vacation spot with a pleasant natural atmosphere. The beautiful and green natural scenery also adds to the attraction, there are also many interesting selfie spots that can be your choice. The viewing post which is also fun can be a reason to visit here.


Location: Karang Patihan Village, Balong, State Forest, Jambon, Ponorogo Regency, East Java
Coban Lawe Waterfall

One of the hidden tourist attractions in Ponorogo is the Coban Lawe Waterfall on the slopes of Mount Wilis.

This waterfall can be said to be hidden but offers a special natural charm. In addition to an impressive waterfall with a fairly heavy water discharge, plus the cool air and scenery around it, anyone who visits this waterfall will be reluctant to leave.


Location: Krisik Village, Mountains, Krisik, Pudak, Ponorogo Regency, East Java
Full Moon Reog Performance

The thing that should not be missed when going to Ponorogo, of course, is watching the Reog show. In the past, Reog performances could only be seen during the Grebeg Suro event or the Reog Festival. However, that has changed. There is a Reog show which is regularly held every full moon night. Therefore, it is called the Full Moon Reog Performance.

To watch this show, tourists can directly come to the main stage area in Ponorogo Square. The show usually starts at 19.30 WIB. If the weather is not favorable, the performance will be moved to Paseban Alun-Alun Ponorogo. This show can be watched for free by the general public.


Location: Ponorogo Square, Mangkujayan, Ponorogo District, Ponorogo Regency, East Java
